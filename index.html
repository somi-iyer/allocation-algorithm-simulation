Algorithm: unweighted max-min (water-filling) fairness. Each round, remaining supply is split equally across dealers whose demand is not yet fully met; any dealer whose remaining demand is below that equal share is capped at their demand, and the leftover is redistributed in the next round. See ALGORITHM.md in this repo for the full write-up.
